BROWN SHAVER

The typical commercial chicken breed, infamous for their dual purpose of eggs and meat. They are an all-rounder breed as they are low-maintenance and friendly, making them an ideal type for first time chicken keepers.


    Eggs
  • -all-year layers, even throughout winter
  • -around 300 eggs per year
  • -small eggs, around a size 5-6
  • -egg sizes are usually a 6, 7 or 8


    Personality:
  • Gentle, docile and calm
  • -friendly and affectionate to humans
  • -fairly quiet birds
  • -prefer company, so at least 2-3 brown shavers in a flock is ideal
  • -poor flying abilities


    Appearance:
  • -fluffy, poofy plumage from head to toe
  • -black skin and tinted bones
  • -black eyes
  • six colours available: white, black, buff, partridge and splash


    • Lifespan: 2-3 years due to the overload in egg laying
