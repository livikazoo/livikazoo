SILKIE

Silkies are known for their fluffy voluminous plumage and their black coloured bones and skin. They are small in size weighing at 1.5-2kgs on average. They were first discovered by Marco Polo when he was travelling in China, and brought them home back to the West.


    Eggs
  • -poor egg layers
  • -100-120 eggs per year
  • -small eggs, around a size 5-6
  • -tint coloured eggs


    Personality:
  • Docile, peaceful and friendly
  • -picked on by other assertive breeds because of their extremely friendliness
  • -usually at the bottom of the pecking order
  • -enjoy interacting with humans
  • -make good broody mothers


    Appearance:
  • -fluffy, poofy plumage from head to toe
  • -black skin and tinted bones
  • -black eyes
  • six colours available: white, black, buff, partridge and splash


    • Lifespan: 8-9 years
