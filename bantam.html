BANTAMS

The Bantam breed is an umbrella term for small sized fowl. Bantam chickens are small in size and typically have feathers on their feet.

    Eggs:
  • -average egg layers
  • -180-220 eggs per year
  • -small eggs, usually a size 5 or 6
  • -egg colour can range from green to blue to cream.


    Personality:
  • Assertive, loyal and calm
  • -friendly to humans overtime
  • -usually top of pecking order due to their assertive nature


    Appearance:
  • -small sized
  • -feathers on legs
  • -wattles and combs are red


    • Lifespan: 5-7 years
